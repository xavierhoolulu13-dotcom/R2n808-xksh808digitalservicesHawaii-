# R2N808 / XKSH808 Digital Services Hawaii
Ultra‑light business plan + repo blueprint for building a Hawaii‑focused AI assistant for freelancers and small businesses.

## 1. BUSINESS OVERVIEW
- Mission: AI assistant for Hawaii freelancers (email cleanup, gig tracking, social media).
- Target Market: Freelancers + small biz owners (20–50), Hawaii‑based.
- Unique Value: Localized AI workflows built for Hawaii culture and freelance realities.

## 2. MARKET RESEARCH
- Trends: Freelance economy rising; remote work; AI productivity tools.
- Customer Profile: Overwhelmed by email, gig chaos, weak social presence.
- Competitors:
  - Molt.ai — email AI, no local context
  - Mailbutler — tools only
  - Hootsuite — social only
  - Otter.ai — transcription only

## 3. BUSINESS MODEL
- Revenue: Freemium → paid tiers; affiliate tools; sponsored content.
- Pricing: Free tier → $5 / $20 monthly upgrades.
- Costs: Hosting (free), marketing (low), dev (free tools).

## 4. MARKETING STRATEGY
- Acquisition: Local events, coworking spaces, FB groups, Reddit, IG.
- Content: Freelance tips, email cleanup, gig tracking, social media guides.
- Social: IG + FB; testimonials; short videos; productivity posts.

## 5. TECH STACK (FREE)
- Website: Wix / WordPress.com
- CRM: HubSpot Free
- Email: Mailchimp / Sendinblue
- Analytics: Google Analytics, FB Insights
- PM: Trello, Notion
- Design: Canva

## 6. LAUNCH CHECKLIST
- Pre‑Launch: Build prototype; landing page; socials; content queue.
- Launch Day: Announce; live demo; press outreach.
- Post‑Launch: Feedback; fixes; user nurturing.

## 7. GROWTH ROADMAP
- 30 Days: 100 users; 50 emails; 10 content pieces.
- 90 Days: 500 users; subscriptions live; 2 partnerships.
- 1 Year: 3000+ users; expanded features; affiliate revenue.
